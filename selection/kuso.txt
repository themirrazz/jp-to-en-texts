[くそ]
Also stylized as: くそー and クソ

NOUN
1. A literal piece of shit.
  Ex: "きもい、床で僕の犬はクソしたよ！"
      "Gross/Ew, my dog shat/shitted/crapped on the floor!"

2. Junk; shit.
  Ex: "何はすべてこのくそ？！"
      "What is all this shit?!"

ADJECTIVE
1. Shitty; of low quality
  Ex: "その映画はクソかった。"
      "That movie was shit."

2. Damned; pesky, or annoying
  Ex: "このくそガキ..."
      "That damn brat..."

EXCLAMATION
1. An exclamation used to express anger.
  Ex: "くそー！"
      "Damn it!/Damn!/Damnit!/Dammit!/Shit!/Fuck!"
